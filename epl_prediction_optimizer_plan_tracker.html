System Architecture

End-to-End Flow

Stage Purpose Inputs Outputs
Stage A Predict match outcome probabilities. Historical results, ClubElo ratings, fixtures, rolling form, optional odds/xG. fixture_probabilities.csv with P(home), P(draw), P(away).
Stage B Optimize contest picks across the season. Fixture probabilities and contest rules. optimized_picks.csv with one selected team per contest week.
Stage C Operate, review, and track performance visually. Model artifacts, predictions, optimized picks, actual results. HTML dashboard with actions, charts, history, and progress tracking.
Contest Rules

Optimization Constraints

Rule Implementation
Eligible days Only Saturday and Sunday matches can become pick candidates.
Weekly pick Exactly one selected team per contest week.
Use every team Each EPL team selected at least once.
Max two uses Each team selected at most twice total.
Venue limit Each team selected at most once at home and at most once away.
